Grilled Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast: 500g
  • Olive oil: 2 tbsp
  • Garlic: 2 cloves, minced
  • Herbs: 1 tsp thyme, 1 tsp rosemary
  • Salt & pepper: to taste

Preparation Steps:

  • Marinate chicken with olive oil, garlic, herbs, salt & pepper for 30 mins.
  • Preheat grill to medium heat.
  • Grill chicken 6-7 mins per side until cooked through.
  • Serve hot with your choice of sides.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces: 500g
  • Onion: 1 large, chopped
  • Tomatoes: 2 medium, pureed
  • Curry powder: 2 tsp
  • Coconut milk: 1 cup
  • Salt & pepper: to taste

Preparation Steps:

  • Sauté onions until golden, add curry powder.
  • Add chicken and brown for 5 mins.
  • Add tomato puree and coconut milk, simmer 20 mins.
  • Serve hot with rice or bread.
♥

Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces: 500g
  • Flour: 1 cup
  • Eggs: 2, beaten
  • Breadcrumbs: 1 cup
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt & pepper: to taste

Preparation Steps:

  • Coat chicken with flour, dip in eggs, then breadcrumbs.
  • Heat oil and fry chicken until golden brown and cooked inside.
  • Drain excess oil and serve hot.
